About Jon H. Rogers

Jon Rogers is a native of Salt Lake City who received his juris doctorate (law degree) from the Vanderbilt University School of Law in Nashville, Tennessee, a school consistently ranked among the top 20 in the nation. Returning to Utah, he spent a year with a small firm in Layton doing mostly collections work.

In the fall of 1994, Jon decided to focus his practice on consumer law. He began a solo practice in the building that once housed St. Mark's Hospital at 800 North on 300 West in Salt Lake City. Jon married shortly after opening his practice. He and his wife, Leslie, have four children.

Over the years, numerous clients had inquired if Jon did estate planning, indicating that basic, affordable estate plans were hard to find. In 2010, Jon Rogers expanded his practice to include estate planning.

Jon Rogers has bench and jury trial experience and regularly speaks to the community on consumer law and estate planning issues. Many of his referrals come from past clients and other attorneys (frequently including opposing counsel).
